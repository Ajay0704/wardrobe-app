# Onboarding quiz research (AJA-35)

Last updated: 2026-07-11

## Why closet apps churn

Closet apps (Stylebook, Whering, Acloset, Indyx, Cladwell) fail at the **catalog wall**: photo + tag dozens–hundreds of items before value. Typical setup is hours; most users abandon after a small handful of items. See [Outflik — Why Every Closet App Fails at the First Step](https://www.outflik.com/blog/why-closet-apps-fail) and Indyx’s own ~4–6 hour estimate ([How it works](https://www.myindyx.com/how-it-works)).

**Implication:** Onboarding must not ask for a full closet. Activation = a few pieces you wear *this week*, then an outfit.

## How styling products quiz

- **Stitch Fix:** Style + lifestyle + fit/budget; moved toward **visual / trade-off** questions and a **StyleFile** payoff (“we get you”) rather than long self-description surveys ([support](https://support.stitchfix.com/support/solutions/articles/153000250443-how-to-create-an-account), [StyleFile rollout](https://newsroom.stitchfix.com/blog/beginning-to-reimagine-stitch-fix/), [case study summary](https://agixtech.com/case-studies/stitch-fix/)).
- **Wantable:** Quiz covers size, lifestyle, budget, style; boxes themed around **real-life occasions** ([How Wantable works](https://www.wantable.com/how-wantable-works/)).

## Mobile onboarding UX

Keep personalization to ~3–5 questions; every answer must change the product; show progress; skip non-essentials; get to first value fast ([Purchasely](https://www.purchasely.com/blog/app-onboarding-process), [Appalize](https://www.appalize.com/blog/app-marketing/mobile-app-onboarding-best-practices-that-reduce-churn), [UXCam](https://uxcam.com/blog/10-apps-with-great-user-onboarding/)).

## Our quiz (in progress — AJA-35)

1. **Goal** — dressed faster / use what I own / plan week / shop smarter  
2. **Occasions** (≤3) — everyday, work, nights out, active, formal → matching tags  
3. **Lean** (1) — relaxed / polished / bold / classic  
4. **Snapshot** — StyleFile-lite → **Enter Wardrobe**

**Activation (not done as a wizard step):** empty **Today** coaches “add what you’d wear this week”; ambient `n/2 for your first look`. Bulk/gallery path and polish still open on AJA-35.
